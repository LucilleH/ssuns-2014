Refund policy

The $90 club registration fee is a one-time non-refundable fee per delegation. If you decide not to join us at SSUNS 2013:

  • Before September 10th, your refund will be the payment received minus the $85 club registration fee.
  • Before October 1st, your refund will be a 50% refund of your Total Delegate Fee.

Please note that after October 1st there are no refunds under any circumstances.
