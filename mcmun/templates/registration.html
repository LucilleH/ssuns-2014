McGill Tours

Are you considering attending one of Canada’s most prestigious universities? Are you interested in learning more about McGill University or simply finding out more about what student life in Montreal is like? SSUNS is also offering McGill tours on the Thursday morning of the conference. The tour will be guided by official tour guides who will be giving one-hour tours of the campus. Delegates will be divided into groups of around 15, and at the end of the tour, delegates will have the opportunity to visit the bookstore and pick up a few souvenirs. For more information, you can visit http://ssuns.org/mcgill-tours). Cost per delegate is $2.

The McGill Tours Application reached the maximum capacity. It is now closed. Contact ops@ssuns.org for more information.
