The deadline for this scholarship application is November 1st.

Scholarships will be distributed to fully registered delegations based on genuine financial need, demonstrated efforts at fundraising destined for covering the costs of attending McMUN as well as the size and costs faced by the attending delegation.

Please note that, in order to be eligible for the scholarship, your delegation needs to be fully registered (with the {% if school.use_tiered %}deposit{% else %}full payment{% endif %} sent) by {% if school.use_priority %}October 1st, the deadline for priority{% else %}November 18st, the deadline for regular{% endif %} registration. Delegations selected to receive the scholarship will be notified by email by December 1st. The amount of the scholarship can be collected in the form of a cheque at the time of the conference.

If you have any questions regarding the scholarship application or selection process, please do not hesitate to contact our Chargé D’Affaires, Daniel Stysis, at charge@mcmun.org.
