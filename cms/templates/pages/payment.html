SSUNS 2013 registration fees consist of 2 components:

    1) "Delegation Fee" - Non-Refundable delegation fee

    2) "Total Delegate Fee" - Number of Delegates x Fee Per Delegate

Please note that the non-refundable delegation fee is one single fee paid per delegation while the total fee per delegate is the cost per delegate applied to each delegate attending SSUNS 2014. The costs for SSUNS 2014 are listed in the table below:

Therefore the total cost of registration is as follows:

    Registration Fee=Delegation Fee + (Number of Delegates × Fee per Delegate)

How to Register

  • All registrations are to be made online through the SSUNS website at ssuns.org/registration-form. If you are unable to complete your registration online, please contact Hilary Chu at schools@ssuns.org, who will be able to assist you with your registration.
  • Please follow all the steps and fill in all the required fields on the registration form
  • Make sure to select your method of payment:
    a) Cheques received by mail
    b) PayPal
  • Please take note of the deadlines for payments listed in the above table following the Priority or Regular Registration timetable.

Additional Notes:

  • Schools will only receive their country and committee assignments once they have paid both their Delegation Fee and Total Delegate Fee in full. Some exemptions may be given in certain circumstances if they are brought up to Hilary Chu at schools@ssuns.org before the deadline for payment
  • Canadian schools are required to pay the entirety of their registration fee in Canadian dollars (CAD).
  • US and international schools are required to pay the entirety of their registration fee in US dollars (USD)
  • The delegation fee is non-refundable upon payment
  • Delegations have until August 31st for a 100% refund on total delegate fees should the registration be cancelled before this date
  • Delegations have until October 6th for a 50% refund on total delegate fees should the registration be cancelled before this date
  • After October 6th, there will no longer be any refunds of any kind
  • For any delegates requiring a visa, a recommendation letter will be provided only once the total registration fee (deposit + total delegate fee) has been paid in full
