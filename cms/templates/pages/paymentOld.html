Registration fee

The McMUN registration fee consists of two parts: the club registration fee, and the per-delegate fee.

Club registration fee

A one-time, non-refundable fee of $75 per delegation, regardless of delegation size.

Per-delegate fee

Priority North American registration Regular North American registration International registration
September 1 - October 1 October 2 - November 18 August 1 - November 18
$80 $95 $50

Please note that both American and overseas schools pay in USD and Canadian schools pay in Canadian dollars. Only schools located outside the US and Canada are considered to be international.

Payment options

Delegations may pay either in full or in a tiered system, consisting of a deposit payment and remainder payment.

The deposit includes the club registration fee and 50% of the total per-delegate fee (75 + 0.5*[per-delegate fee]*[number of delegates]).

Priority registration: deposit received by October 1st.

Regular and international registration: deposit received by November 18th.

All remainder payments must be received by December 1st.

Payment methods

The preferred payment method is through online payment (via Payment). You can choose your desired payment method when registering. Note that there is a 3% convenience fee for online payment for schools that have registered after September 1st. Refund policy

The $75 club registration fee is a one-time non-refundable fee per delegation.

If you decide not to join us at McMUN 2013:

  • Before November 18th, your refund will be the payment received minus the club registration fee.
  • Before January 1st, your refund will be the payment received minus the deposit.

Please note that after January 1st there are no refunds under any circumstances.
