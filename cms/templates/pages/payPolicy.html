    1) Non-Refundable delegation fee

    2) Total fee per delegate attending

Please note that the non-refundable delegation fee is one single fee paid per delegation while the total fee per delegate is the cost per delegate applied to each delegate attending SSUNS 2013. The costs for SSUNS 2013 are listed in the table below:

    Registration Fee=Delegation Fee + (Number of Delegates × Fee per Delegate)

How to Register

Upon registration, delegations are required to submit:

    1) Completed registration form
    2) Deposit (Non-refundable delegation fee)

Additional Notes:

  • Canadian schools are required to pay the entirety of their registration fee in Canadian dollars (CAD). To be eligible for Priority Registration Fees, all payments (via Cheque or PayPal) must be submitted before July 30th
  • US and international schools are required to pay the entirety of their registration fee in US dollars (USD) or Canadian dollars (CAD)
  • The delegation fee is non-refundable upon payment
  • Delegations have until September 10th for a 100% refund on total delegate fees
  • Delegations have until October 1st for a 50% refund on total delegate fees
  • After October 8th, there will no longer be any refunds of any kind
  • For any delegates requiring a visa, a recommendation letter will be provided only once the total registration fee (deposit + total delegate fee) has been paid in full
