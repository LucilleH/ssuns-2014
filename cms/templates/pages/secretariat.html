Secretary-General

Returning to the secretariat for his third and final time as this year’s Secretary-General, Nikhil Garg. No one seems to embody his own words, “SSUNS is a lifestyle,” better than himself. In addition to eating, sleeping, and breathing SSUNS, Nikhil finds the time to pursue a joint honours degree in Economics and Finance. Well-travelled, adventurous, and always seeking a new challenge, Nikhil has ventured to destinations as exotic as Cape Town and Vietnam, and has lived in India, Indonesia, and the Netherlands prior to settling in Montreal. With years of Model UN experience under his belt, Nikhil is determined to lead this year’s secretariat in taking SSUNS to new and unprecedented heights. (Both figuratively and literally – he summited Mt. Kilimanjaro in seven days.) What draws Nikhil back to SSUNS, year after year, you may ask? His interactions with you – delegates and staff – are what he finds to be the most rewarding aspect of all of the diligent work that he puts into this conference!
